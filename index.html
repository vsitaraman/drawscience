Our Story

"Put the art in article." Draw Science was founded to connect the world with cutting-edge research, in an accessible & entertaining medium. What started as a student blog grew into an internationally crowdfunded campaign to revolutionize science communication.

From 2014 – 2016, we hope we did exactly that. With our work often featured on sites like VICE & Newsweek, our science infographics have amassed over 3 million views. More importantly, we've been able to spread the vision around the world: from the Lindau-Nobel Conferences in Germany to featuring on Forbes.
